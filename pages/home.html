Mission Statement:
"Innovating healthcare for everyone."

Vision Statement:
"To reduce sports-related injury new and seasoned athletes by increasing accessibility to health professionals."

Who We Are: UHealth is a nonprofit organization devoted to
ensuring athletes of all experience levels can easily be in
contact with a health professional in order to prevent or
recover from sports-related injuries.



What We Do: By creating a simple and interactive interface, users
can log their information regarding exercise habits, medical
history, and general health. With our immediate feedback and
notification settings, your local physician will be able to
comment on whether you are at risk for any sports-related
injuries, as well as providing instant feedback and assigning
tasks on how to recover from injuries--all at the comfort in
your own home! Don’t have a doctor or physical therapist?
No problem: our site will match you with a certified
professional in your area.
